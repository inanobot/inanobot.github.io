DNA Origami Linker

14
Feb

Our dirty lab work...shh!

Last updated 25.09.16

Materials needed:

  • 1.5 mL Eppendorf tubes
  • Pipettes
  • Pipette Tips
  • 1M tris(2-carboxyethyl)phosphine (TCEP)
  • Single-stranded DNA 5'ThioMC6-D/TTTTTTTCTTTGTTTCTTT
  • Liquid nitrogen
  • Biotin-maleimide
  • MilliQ Water
  • N-Methyl-2-pyrrolidone (NMP)
  • 0.1 mM KH2PO4 (Potassium Dihydrogen Phosphate) buffer
  • Vortex mixer
  • Centrifuge
  • Sample Rotator
  • Ultra Performance Liquid Chromatography (UPLC) machine
  • Mass Spectrometer
  • Speed vacuum

Procedure:

  1. Ensure that all Eppendorf tubes and pipette tips are all low-bind and low-retention respectively
  3. Pipette 200μL of 100μM ssDNA for modification
  5. Prepare a three-fold excess of 1M TCEP
  7. Dilute the TCEP sample to 1:100
  9. Add 6 μL of TCEP to the DNA
  11. Let the sample incubate at room temperature for 30 minutes
  13. Dissolve 1mg of biotin-maleimide in 1mL NMP
  15. Add 90.3 μL of dissolved biotin-maleimide and add it to the DNA
  17. Check the pH of the sample and make sure it is approximately 7.2
  19. Add 10 μL of 0.1 mM potassium dihydrogen phosphate buffer at a time until pH reaches 7.2
  21. Place sample in a rotator overnight
  23. Run UPLC and Mass Spectrometry on a 10μL aliquot of the sample
  24. If product is obtained, purify the sample using High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and place the sample in a speed vacuum set at 0.1 mbar
