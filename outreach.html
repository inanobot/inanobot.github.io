Foundation of ISCA (International Student Competition Association) Dresden

TU Dresden

Each year students from TU Dresden are interested in participating in Biomod and each team needs to go through the same steps. This year we took the initiative to streamline the organization process of setting up a team through the university. This association signifies the creation of a shared movement with the intention to share experience, connections, and collected knowledge future participants. The association is open and everyone is warmly welcome.

Long Night of Science

CRTD

Long Night of Science hosted by TU Dresden was one of the first opportunities for us to present our project to the Dresden community. The feedback was incredible and we can’t thank the Dresden community enough! We set up a stall in the CRTD and had around 750 people visit us.

We had something for everyone at our stall. Our maze was designed for children, but enjoyed by everyone. Exactly 330 balloons were popped that night and our custom designed Nanorover-Pacman game attracted 427 players. We took great pleasure in sharing our project and hope you liked our cupcakes!

Foundation of ISCA (International Student Competition Association) Dresden

TU Dresden

Each year students from TU Dresden participating in BIOMOD go through the same steps. This year we finally found the way to organise it and to make competitions like BIOMOD not only a competition, but the social stream. International Student Competition Association was founded so that all experience, collected knowledge, funds and connections will be be shared with future participants.
